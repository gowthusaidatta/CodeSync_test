About CodeSync

CodeSync is an innovative platform designed to empower educational institutions and students alike. We provide a seamless way to synchronize, monitor, and analyze student coding profiles from leading competitive programming sites and GitHub. Our mission is to foster a data-driven approach to student development, helping them achieve their full potential in the ever-evolving world of programming.
